Group Five Guys Presents

LONDON BICYCLE TRAVEL

POST-COVID-19

Analysis

Our analysis is implemented in the London area, and it includes three parts. At first, we investigated the COVID-19 infection trend and different transport demands. Next, we further explore bicycle travel by analyzing bicycle flow/duration change trends and comparing daily bicycle flow/duration. Finally, regression analysis was implemented to check the coronavirus impact on bicycle travel and build a SARIMAX model to predict bicycle travel.
