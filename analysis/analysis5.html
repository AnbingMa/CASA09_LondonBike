Bike Flow/Duration in 2019 vs 2020-2021

Day Duration 2019 vs 2020-2021

According to the first figure, before COVID-19, there was a significant peak time of the user duration of bikes during the daytime, as well as a decreased use time at night. After the burst out of the pandemic, this characteristic became obviously, but a decreasing using duration in wintertime appeared (from 2020-07 to 2021-01) . However, the total flow counts were higher than before, the range of Y was 0.5e8 to 1.25e8, which was 0.1e8 to 0.8e8 before, which indicates people can accept long-distance using bikes for commuting.
