Covid Borough in 2019 vs 2020 vs 2021

COVDI-19 total cases in boroughs

We choose one typical day from three years to visualize the spatial distribution and changes of covid cases. From the map, we could see that the covid cases are greater in inner London in 2020 at the beginning of the pandemic. Especially the Kensington and Chelsea borough has the highest total covid cases on a typical day in 2020. However, the high covid cases show a tendency to spread outward. In 2021, Borough Newham which has significant problems with poverty and inequality and borough Redbridge experienced higher total covid cases than any other boroughs.
