Reression analysis

Bicycle day flow & COVID-19 new cases

Regression analysis was implemented to examine whether the number of COVID-19 infections had an effect on people's choice of bicycle trips, and all the variables are normalized in the same range to compare the independent variable impact through coefficient. The analysis showed that the number of COVID-19 infections was negatively correlated with the flow of bicycle trips, taking into account weather conditions, but this impact was small compared with weather factors.

On the other hand, It is found that the bicycle travel flow and daily covid-19 infection are both increased in the covid-19 period. So it could be supposed that other factors like policies, limited the COVID-19 impact on bicycle travel.
