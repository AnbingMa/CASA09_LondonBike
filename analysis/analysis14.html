Based on the analysis of the data, it was found that there was a rapid increase in bicycles during COVID-19, for which we set up some scenarios and suggestions for the futural needs of Londoners.

Easy-park, as a usual tool in daily life

As the use of bicycles becomes more common, parking facilities should be managed. Parking infrastructure includes cycle racks on the pavement, which can be sheltered or uncovered; protected stations to prevent theft and infractions; and cycle lockers, usually located at public transport stops.

Various programs for children and adults to improve their cycling skills and knowledge of cycling-related laws are available.

Improve London's cycling facilities and create a network of connected, high quality and easily accessible cycle routes for all Londoners.
