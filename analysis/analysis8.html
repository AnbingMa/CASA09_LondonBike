Daily cycle changed travel time in three years

Except for the daily flows, we are also curious about the total duration of cycle usage. From the picture, the total usage time of the public cycle still follows the same pattern of the flow count with two peaks at rush hour in 2019 and 2021 while only peaks around afternoon rush hour in 2020.

Bicycle travel time during morning peak hours has also been reduced to about half of the pre-pandemic total in 2019. But, the total riding time in the afternoon peak hours in 2020 has exceeded that in both 2019 and 2021. Non-essential contact and travel are restricted while parks are opened for outdoor walking and cycling during lockdown which helps explain the much larger increase in cycling from 16:00 to 18:00 during the pandemic period compared to before the pandemic.
