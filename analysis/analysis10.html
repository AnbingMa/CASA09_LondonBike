Daily Flow/duration

Changed travel time on a typical week in three years

The duration of trips on weekdays and weekends follows the same pattern as the number of trips.

However, the peak riding time on weekday afternoon rush hours in 2020 topped that of 2019, setting a new record. And the size is larger on weekends since the highest total riding hours during peak time on weekends is 15 million minutes compared to only 5 million minutes on weekdays. In addition, the Peak riding hours on weekend in 2020 is more than five times higher than in pre-pandemic 2019, which further indicates the increasing demand and usage of the public cycle during the pandemic time.
